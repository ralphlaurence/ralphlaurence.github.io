"I love how she makes me feel.. like anything's possible, or like life is worth it." -Tom Hansen

Dear Love


Of all the people that i have met, talked, bonded, fell in loved with, i have never thought that i would be with you. and because of that, i am so grateful for all the occasions that led me to this moment, living life with your love. i never regretted any disastrous fights nor hateful rants we said to each other, for every smack of a chisel that we carved into our reltionship, it will become a hard-earned and well-desereved outcome of an art. I love you and i will always love you dearly, even though there are times i can't express my feelings through words and gifts, i convey those emotions by actions that tells you how much i love you, much as it is impossible to measure my love in just three words, i wish i can express it through the deepest, most alluring, most sincere action that i can show, i will do it everyday for the rest of my life. May we treasure our time together until grow apart.

yours truly, RL
